All About Dogs

What Makes Dogs So Lovable?

Dogs are widely regarded as man's best friend, and it's not hard to see why. They are loyal, affectionate, and always happy to see you.

Not only do they make great companions, but dogs also have a variety of other talents, including being trained as service animals, search and rescue dogs, and even therapy dogs.
